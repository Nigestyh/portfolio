    About Me

    I am Nigesty, a self-taught front-end developer. My interest in coding began in the second half of 2023 and quickly grew into a passion. I’ve been teaching myself ever since. What sparked my interest in software development was how naturally it engaged my logical and solution-oriented mind. As a creative person, I found the process of designing an app, programming it, and bringing it to life both thrilling and fulfilling.

    I’ve learned through resources such as SheCodes, Scrimba, GeeksforGeeks, The Odin Project, and MDN Web Docs, focusing on HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React. Building real projects has given me practical experience in creating dynamic, responsive, and interactive web applications.
    I am especially interested in creating systems that are efficient, user-friendly, innovative, and have a positive impact.

    I value continuous learning and collaboration, and I’m committed to enhancing my skills as I work toward a professional career in software development.
